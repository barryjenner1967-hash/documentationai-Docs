## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="What You'll Need">
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account registration
- A few minutes to set up your first task
</Callout>

Mind Dump is a web-based application, so no downloads or installations are required. Simply use any device with internet access.

## Create Account and Log In

Follow these steps to access your Mind Dump dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Mind Dump" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to [https://minddump.uk](https://minddump.uk).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register or Log In" icon="user">
    On the login page, enter your username and password.
    
    New users: Use the registration form to create an account with your email.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Check "Keep me logged in for 7 days" for convenience on personal devices.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Login" icon="check-circle">
    Click **ğŸ” Login**. If using a shared computer, always log out afterward to protect your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
