title: How to Use This Manual
---
To get the most out of your training and this documentation, we recommend navigating through the materials in the following order:

- **Introduction:** We highly recommend starting here to gain a clear understanding of the overarching aims and philosophy behind Mind Dump.

- **Quick Start Guide:** Designed specifically for new users, this section walks you through your crucial first steps. It will help you log in, create your first tasks, recall information, and edit task data. The Quick Start Guide also covers essential configuration settings, explains how to create and edit tags and task notes, and rounds off by showing you how Custom Fields are created and used.

- **Guides:** Once you are comfortable with the basics, move to this section. Here, we explore all of the platform's advanced features in depth. These guides aim to continuously enhance your understanding and maximise the potential of your Mind Dump workspace.

- **Core Concepts:** This reference section explains the background and architecture behind the specific concepts used in Mind Dump. If you ever encounter terms or structural ideas that are unfamiliar or unclear, this section is the best place to explore them.

**Navigating the Documentation:** This manual is extensively cross-referenced to help you build a complete understanding of the system. You will find hyperlinks throughout the text that connect practical, step-by-step feature explanations directly back to their underlying core concepts.
