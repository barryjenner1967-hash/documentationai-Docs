## Creating a Notebook

To organize your notes, create a new notebook:

1. In the Mind Dump dashboard, click the **Notebooks** tab.
2. Click the **New Notebook** button.
3. Enter a name for your notebook.
4. (Optional) Add a description or tags.
5. Click **Create** to finish.

Your new notebook will appear in the list, ready to add notes.

<Callout kind="info">
Notebooks help you keep related notes together and improve searchability.
</Callout>
