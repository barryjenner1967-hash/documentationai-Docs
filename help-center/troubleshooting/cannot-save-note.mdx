---
title: Unable to Save Note
description: Quick fix if notes aren't saving in Mind Dump.
---

## Issue
- The **Save** button is disabled, or changes aren't saved.

## Possible Causes
- Internet connection lost
- Account not authenticated

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    ````
    Reload the page and make sure you are online.
    If needed, reconnect your internet and retry.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in again">
    ````
    Click your profile icon and select **Log Out**, then log in again.
    This refreshes your session.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
